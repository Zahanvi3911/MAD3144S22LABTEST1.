RABBIT:rabbit, any of 29 species of long-eared mammals belonging to the family Leporidae, excluding hares

Rabbit:Rabbits are ground dwellers that live in environments ranging from desert to tropical forest and wetland.

Rabbit:The long ears of rabbits are most likely an adaptation for detecting predators. In addition to their prominent ears, which can measure up to 6 cm (more than 2 inches) long, rabbits have long, powerful hind legs and a short tail.
